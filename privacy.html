Privacy Policy

Last updated 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our online video watermark removal service (the "Service").

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us.

Our Service is designed to process videos entirely within your web browser. Your videos are not uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers. All video decoding, watermark removal, and re-encoding take place locally on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We intentionally do not collect or store:

  • Your original video files
  • Your processed video files
  • User accounts or login information
  • Payment information (the Service does not process payments)
  • Any information contained within your videos

Since your files never leave your device, we cannot access or view your content.

3. How the Service Works

The Service uses modern browser technologies, including WebCodecs and the HTML Canvas API, to process videos locally.

When you select a video:

  • The file remains on your device.
  • Processing occurs entirely inside your browser.
  • The processed video is generated locally.
  • The output file is downloaded directly to your device.

No copies of your videos are sent to or stored by us.

4. Technical Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect limited technical information necessary to operate and secure the website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of requests
  • Requested pages
  • Basic diagnostic and security logs

This information is used solely for website operation, security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

We do not use this information to identify individual users or build user profiles.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The Service does not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Your browser may store a small amount of local data (such as interface preferences or selected options) using local storage. This information remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service is designed to minimize third-party requests.

If we introduce analytics, advertising, or other third-party services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and where required by law, your consent will be requested before such technologies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Because we do not collect or store your video files or personal account information, there is generally no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete.
